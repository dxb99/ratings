How Ratings Work

Each voting cycle starts fresh. Old votes from previous cycles are not reused.

Each player is rated in six categories: Combat Skills, Communication / Status Updates, Decision Making, Map Awareness, Movement / Speed, and Team Impact.

Low = 0 | Fair = 2.5 | Average = 5 | Good = 7.5 | Excellent = 10

Untouched sliders are not counted. Every category must be rated before submitting.

The system averages the six category scores for each player. That decimal number becomes the player's overall skill level used by the matchup generator.
